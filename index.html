Hello! I am Gabriel and you are about to know me a bit better

The first steps

I was born in October 31th, 1993 in a southern brazilian town called Florianopolis. As a child I was very agitated, slept little and had a lot of energy to spend. That led my father to enroll me in all different kind of sports such as taekwondo, football, horseback riding, and others.

Around 8 years old, my family and I moved to another city. This caused me to lose touch with my friends and classmates so I began to spend more time at home playing video games and computer games.

It was at this time that I became fascinated by Computer Science.

Education and Professional Background

I ended up studying Business Management after successfully passing the entrance examination at a very prestigious Federal University.

I finished my bachelor degree in 2019 at Federal University of Santa Catarina and have done internships and worked since I started my studies in 2013.

The biggest companies I have worked for were Vail Resorts Retail and Engie.

Germany And Other Challenges

In May of 2021 me and my wife moved to Germany. After months looking for a job I was able to get one but it wasn't exactly related to the field I was looking for so it didn't last long, although it was an amazing experience where I got to work with incredible people.

After another couple of months looking for other jobs I found myself asking some questions like "Is this what I really want to do?".

And from that moment on I began to study Web Development on my own. And I have been on this journey ever since January of 2022.

The next steps

I discovered that my purpose is to work with Information Technology, because it's something that drives me forward always looking to improve my skill sets and my communication habilities.

At the moment I have a base foundation in HTML, CSS and JavaScript but I won't stop learning.

Changing my career path was and still is a challenge but I am proud of the decision I made and can't wait to show the world my potential.
